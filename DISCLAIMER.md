# Disclaimer

FB Stealth is an unofficial browser extension and is not affiliated with,
endorsed by, sponsored by, or connected to Meta, Facebook, or Messenger.

Use this extension at your own risk. Facebook may change its web internals at
any time, which can break features, reduce effectiveness, or affect normal
site behavior.

FB Stealth is provided as a best-effort privacy tool. It does not guarantee
that every seen receipt, story view, typing signal, or related Facebook
activity signal will be blocked in every situation.
